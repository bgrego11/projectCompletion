About Project Completion

The Goal of Project Completion is to provide our clients with affordable Handyman Services where you can trust the quality of work from an experienced source. The company was founded by current CEO, Kevin Holmes.

Kevin has been working as a licensed contractor in New York for over 15 years. He has been a certified electrician for the NYC Department of Environmental Protection for over 25 years and has been a real estate investor for more than 25 years as well.

Kevin is an active member of the community. He is always encouraging positive growth and development for those around him. He mentors local youth and delivers motivational speeches to young people about reaching their goals and mapping out their future.

Entrust Kevin with your most important investment. You can contact him for an estimate to solve your household needs or you can sign up for an annual maintenance plan to keep your home running smoothly. You can count on Kevin to treat your home as if it is his own.
