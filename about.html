About Project Completion

The Goal of Project Completion is to provide the people of the Carolinas with affordable Handyman Services where you can trust the quality of work from an experienced source. The company was founded by current CEO Kevin Holmes

After graduating from George Westinghouse Vocational School, Kevin Holmes has been working as a licensed contractor for over 15 years. Among his other credentials he was a certified electrician for the Department of Environmental Protection and has been a real estate investor for 25 years.

Kevin has also been an active member of the community, always encouraging postive growth and development for those sround him. He mentors local youth and delivers motivational speeches to young people about reaching their goals
